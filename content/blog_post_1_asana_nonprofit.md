# How to Set Up Asana for a Nonprofit: Complete Guide (2026)

**By [Your Name] | Business Operations & SaaS Implementation Expert**

Nonprofits face a unique challenge: you need to manage programs, track grants, coordinate volunteers, and report to donors — usually with a fraction of the staff and budget that for-profit organizations have.

I've implemented Asana for 20+ nonprofits, and the pattern is always the same: **spreadsheets are killing your productivity.**

## Why Asana (Free Tier Is Enough)

- Unlimited tasks and projects
- Up to 15 team members
- List, Board, and Calendar views
- 100+ integrations

## The 4-Team Structure

1. **Programs** — Track your mission delivery
2. **Fundraising** — Grant pipeline, events, campaigns
3. **Operations** — Board, volunteers, staff, finance
4. **Marketing & Communications** — Social, newsletter, website

## The Grant Pipeline (Most Important Project)

Sections: RESEARCH → IN PROGRESS → SUBMITTED → AWARDED → DECLINED → REPORTING DUE
